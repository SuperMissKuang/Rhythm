Privacy Policy

Overview

Rhythm is a menstrual cycle tracking application that allows users to notify trusted contacts (partners, family members, friends) about cycle phase changes via SMS. This feature helps users communicate their needs during different phases of their cycle.

SMS Opt-In Terms of Service Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

The following data is collected and stored locally on your device only:

  • Menstrual Cycle Data: Period start/end dates, cycle length, phase information
  • Self-Care Activities: Activity entries, dates, and times
  • Mood/Wellness Data: Anxiety entries, triggers, and severity levels
  • Contact Information: Names and phone numbers for SMS notifications (if you use this feature)

How We Use Your Information

Information Type Purpose Storage Location
Cycle & Health Data Track your menstrual cycle, display insights, detect phase changes Your device only
Self-Care/Mood Data Track wellness activities and patterns Your device only
Contact Phone Numbers Send SMS notifications to your designated contacts Your device; transmitted to SMS provider only when sending

SMS Notifications & Mobile Information

If you use the "Notify Friends" feature, phone numbers are transmitted to our service providers solely to deliver messages you request:

  • Supabase: Secure cloud function that processes SMS requests
  • Twilio: SMS delivery service

Phone numbers are used only for message delivery and are not permanently stored on external servers.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes.

Recipients must provide explicit consent by replying "YES" before receiving notifications. Recipients can opt-out at any time by replying "STOP".

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Information is only shared in these limited circumstances:

  • SMS Delivery: Phone numbers are transmitted to Supabase/Twilio solely for delivering messages you request
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or court order

What We Don't Collect

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No account credentials (no login required)

Your Rights & Control

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Export: Download all your data as a file anytime
  • Delete: Remove data in app settings or by deleting the app
  • SMS Opt-out: Recipients can reply STOP to any message
