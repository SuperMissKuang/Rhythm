# How Do End-Users Consent to Receive Messages? (40-2048 characters)

**IMPORTANT:** Use this exact text, which references your documentation:

```
The Rhythm app user selects a contact from their phone's native contact picker. Before sending any opt-in request, the app displays a confirmation dialog asking the user if they want to send the request. Upon confirmation, the recipient receives an SMS: "Rhythm: You've been invited to receive cycle updates for support. Reply YES to opt-in. Reply STOP to decline. Msg & data rates may apply."

The recipient must reply YES via SMS to confirm consent. Only after receiving this explicit YES reply are they marked as "confirmed" in the app and eligible to receive cycle notifications. The app tracks opt-in status (pending/confirmed/declined) and only sends notifications to confirmed contacts.

Recipients can opt-out at any time by replying STOP or by being removed by the app user. No messages are sent without prior confirmed consent.

Complete documentation with screenshots: https://supermisskuang.github.io/Rhythm/opt-in-documentation.html
```
